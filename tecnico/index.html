Welcome to the Eastwood Project

The presentation combines elements of comedy and creativity with the aim of representing a futuristic western setting. This project was conceived and executed by the SESI Hortobots team with a focus on innovation and educational entertainment.

Our Robots

Al Byte Gorithm

Our main robot and the antagonist of the show, A.I. Byte Gorithm uses computer vision and gesture recognition to interact with the scene and performers. Built with MDF and Mecanum wheels, it combines AI, precision, and autonomy to deliver dynamic stage actions.

Quezadilhas

A line-following robot riding a black horse, Quezadilhas uses sensors to navigate and trigger a mechanical arm that collapses the prison, symbolically freeing the programmers during the show.

TNT trigger

A compact robot representing a TNT detonator, theatrically activated during a key scene to enhance dramatic tension. It interacts with the stage via sensor-based triggers and adds a dynamic visual moment by simulating the destruction of the prison.

Resources Used

Locomotion

The robots use Mecanum wheels for omnidirectional movement, allowing greater flexibility and precision on stage. This system enables smooth navigation and complex maneuvers in limited spaces.

Internal Structure

Robots are built with MDF sheets (3mm, 6mm) and modular bases, providing durability, easy maintenance, and structural stability. The removable robot base facilitates quick repairs and modular upgrades.

Autonomy and Battery

The robots run on an 11.1V LiPo battery, with extra power from a USB-connected notebook. A backup system and voltage regulator ensure stable, uninterrupted performance throughout the show.
