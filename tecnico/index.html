Welcome to the Eastwood Project

The presentation combines elements of comedy and creativity with the aim of representing a futuristic western setting. This project was conceived and executed by the SESI Hortobots team with a focus on innovation and educational entertainment.

Our Robots

Al Byte Gorithm

Our main robot, inspired by western heroes, combines artificial intelligence with mechanical precision to face challenges with style.

Quezadilhas

Once again, Quezadilhas underwent more changes. This time, mounted on his black horse Brad Piche, he follows a line using his reflectance sensors until he reaches another part of the scenario where, by means of an ultrasonic sensor, he activates a robotic arm that pushes the prison and 'frees' the programmers in the story.

TNT trigger

A robot representing a TNT trigger used in one of the scenes. It is theatrically activated to add excitement to the narrative.

Resources Used

Locomotion

Both the main robot and the secondary robots use differential traction wheels to ensure precision and maneuverability.

Internal Structure

The robot's body was built using lightweight and durable materials, such as expanded PVC and aluminum sheets, aiming for both stability and practicality.
